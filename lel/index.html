Message From Secretary General

Assalamualaikum , delegates It’s my distinct honor and pleasure to welcome you all to the inaugural edition of Bahria Islamabad Model United Nations. BIMUN’15 was a dream project for me and none of it would’ve been possible without my amazing and very patient team, friends and school administration. After grueling hours of hard work and at the verge of losing my vocal chords we bring you the perfect essence of all that any delegate could ever expect to gain from an MUN. True to the spirit of Model UN, we bring you a battle royale between the best and the fiercest debaters from all over Pakistan. Our socials, dare I say, shall serve to show that we bahrians mean business. But we’re not here to debate, socialize for a couple of days win an award and then forget about it. Our aim this year to ‘pursue perpetual peace’ (talk about a tongue twister) is about how we look at things, our perspectives. We hope to change those perspectives, dedicated to the proposition that a single mind influenced could work to be the agent of change in our society. Special shout out to the first timers: You’re the only one not bound by a set perspective on how or what to debate about in a committee, take advantage of that. I personally believe it’s the fresh-minded delegates that are the heart and soul of the committee. There’s nothing a little faith, trust, a lot of research and a bit of pixie dust can’t help with. So bring your A-game! Can’t wait to see you in September! May the best diplomat win! Regards, Arooj Fatima Atiq
